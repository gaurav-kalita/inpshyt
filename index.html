Why
Councelling?

First of all, we need to know that counselling is not mere “advice giving” as most people perceive it to be. It is an intervention of scientific and modern day approach that deals with day to day mental health issues. Lack of proper knowledge about the profession makes people ignore the importance of counselling and its need in facilitating a person to become a better version of himself/herself. Counselling provided by trained professionals can make a profound impact on the lives of individuals, families and communities. So if at any point of time the question “why counselling?” pops up in your mind, register yourself and find a proper answer to your question.
